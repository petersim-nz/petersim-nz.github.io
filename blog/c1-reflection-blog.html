  • What's your take on the DBC/EDA experience?
    It's a different way of learning. It's also obvious that I'm only going to gain from this what I'm prepared to put in.
  • What are your impressions?
    It's going to be really hard work, and really demanding.
  • How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?
    I liked the kitchen metaphor in the video. I'm going to push myself to look at how I can contribute to the whole learning experience.
  • Have your expectations of EDA changed? If so, how?
    Not that much. I went to "A Day in the Life" in Auckland, plus I've talked with a graduate. I think those things have helped me to prepare.
  • Are you excited to participate in this kind of learning environment? Does it make you nervous?
    Definitely excited. I think there's always a fear of the unknown and how I'm going to be able to measure up. Bring it on!
